Crystal Collector Game


How to play:


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. To start the game, click on one of the four crystals.
  3. Each crystal has a different point value attached to it, and when you click it, it will add that amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. Each time the game starts over, the crystals will have new values.
